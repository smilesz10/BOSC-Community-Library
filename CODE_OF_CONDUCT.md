# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
[cite_start]We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation[cite: 20].

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
* Using welcoming and inclusive language.
* Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
* Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
* The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
* [cite_start]Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks[cite: 20].
* Public or private harassment.

## Enforcement
Responsibilities of community maintainers include clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and taking appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.
