# Proposal for Community Library Integration

**To:** Ministry of Education / Local Education Office  
**Subject:** Digital Library Access for Offline Communities

## 1. The Goal
To deploy the BOSC Community Library as a standard resource tool for schools with limited internet connectivity.

## 2. Key Advantages
* **Localized Content:** Unlike global libraries, this system is categorized specifically for our local curriculum and languages (Luganda/Swahili).
* **Data Efficiency:** Students can browse educational manuals and safety guides without consuming expensive mobile data bundles.

## 3. Partnership Request
We seek the endorsement of the local education office to list this library as a recommended digital resource for secondary school students in the region.
